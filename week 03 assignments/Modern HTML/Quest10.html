HTML vs HTML5

HTML HTML5
HTML required several plugins like Adobe flash to embed audio and videos on the webpage. In HTML5 audio and video tags were introduced which made embedding of medias comparitvely very simple.
Less semantic tags were there in HTML. Semantic tags like header and footer were introduced.
HTML didn't have support for local storage. APIs were introduced in HTML5 which made easier to support location and storage of the user.
Less accessibility features. Accessibility is the primary aspect in HTML5.
